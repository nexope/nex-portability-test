# Background and non-overlap statement

This proof-of-concept was initiated on 6 July 2026 as a distinct exploratory asset for a possible NGI Fediversity application.

It contains no domain-specific calculation, forecasting, optimisation, digital-twin integration, project-specific adapter, federated-learning workflow, platform integration or pilot data from other NEXOPE workstreams.

The reference service is intentionally generic. Its only purpose is to exercise deterministic state export, schema validation, restore verification and NixOS packaging.

If a Fediversity proposal is submitted, its repositories, issues, working time, deliverables and accounting records must remain separate from any other NEXOPE funding application, client workstream or internal product. No task or result may be charged to more than one programme.
